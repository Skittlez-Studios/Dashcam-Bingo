Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Dashcam Bingo mobile application (herein referred to as "the Application") created by Skittlez Studios. The Application is provided as an Ad-Supported service intended for use "as is".

1. Information Collection and Use

While the Application does not require user accounts or direct personal information (such as your name or email address), it uses third-party Software Development Kits (SDKs) provided by Google to display advertisements and analyze app performance.

These third-party services collect data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob

2. Data Safety Disclosure

In compliance with the Google Play Data Safety requirements, the following information is collected and shared via the Google AdMob SDK:

  • Approximate Location: Derived from your IP address to show relevant regional ads.
  • Device Identifiers: Including the Advertising ID (AAID) for ad targeting, frequency capping, and fraud prevention.
  • App Activity: Interactions with ads, such as clicks or video views.
  • Diagnostics: Technical crash logs and performance data to ensure the Application remains stable and secure.

3. Data Deletion & User Rights

We do not host your data on any private servers. All data collection is handled by Google’s AdMob infrastructure. You can exercise your right to delete or restrict your data by:

  • Resetting or deleting your Advertising ID through your device settings: Settings > Google > Ads > Reset/Delete Advertising ID.
  • Opting out of personalized ads in your Google Account settings.
  • Contacting us via the email below for any specific privacy-related requests.

4. Security and Retention

Data transmitted off-device is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS protocols. Google retains data such as Ad IDs and crash reports according to its own retention policies, typically for as long as necessary to provide ad services and analytics.

5. Children’s Privacy

We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). The Application is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe data has been collected incorrectly, please contact us immediately.

6. International Users (GDPR & CCPA)

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and California have specific rights to access, update, or delete their personal data. You may limit the use of your information for advertising purposes via your Android device settings.
